---
title: Plane bending
description: A plane cantilever is subjected to a transverse load at the tip.
---


The shear stress field generated by a shear force, $V_y$, directed along $y$, 
applied at the centroid recovers the following parabolic field
$$
\tau_{x z}=0, 
\quad
\tau_{x y}=\frac{6 V_y}{L_x L_y^3}\left(\frac{L_y^2}{4}-y^2\right)
$$
